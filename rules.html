EarthMC Rules: Law of Nations

1. Cheating, server exploits and alternative accounts.

  • 1.1 Cheat clients and other modifications. Any form of cheat client, macro keys, auto clicking and other modifications that gives the player gameplay leverage over other players are not allowed. You are not allowed to use any form of auto clicker. Players found breaking this rule will face a permanent ban. This rule does not apply to:
    • Official EarthMC client.
    • Optifine and other performance improvement mods.
    • Armor and effect status HUD mods.
    • Minimap mods unless they display more information than our Dynmap provides.
    • Purely aesthetic mods.
  • 1.2 Assisting others in breaking server rules. Players found assisting other players in breaking any of the rules listed on this page may be permanently banned.
  • 1.3 Technical errors and server exploits. Players are not allowed to take advantage of apparent server bugs or otherwise exploit technical oversights. Players found taking advantage of bugs or technical oversights without notifying staff may be permanently banned.
  • 1.4 Alternative or stolen accounts. Alternative accounts or “alts” for short are not allowed. You may only use one account per player. Alternative accounts will be banned and the player’s main account may be warned if this rule is deliberately broken. Please contact server staff if you wish to change main account. Playing on a stolen account whether stolen from another server member or acquired from a service distributing hacked accounts will result in an immediate ban for that account.
  • 1.5 Intentionally causing lag or disrupting EarthMC services. Players intentionally causing harm to the services we provide may be permanently banned.

2. Behavior and chat

  • 2.1 TP killing. TP killing is the act of tricking a player in to accepting your teleportation request with the purpose of killing them. Although frowned upon it is still allowed as it is up to each player whether or not to accept the teleportation request.
  • 2.2 Stealing and raiding players/towns/nations. Stealing and raiding other players is allowed. It’s up to each and every player to protect their assets.
  • 2.3 Spam. Spamming or sending messages in a way that disrupts chat flow, normally by sending a similar message repeatedly, is not allowed. Spammers may be warned and temporarily muted. Time muted range between 15 minutes to days depending on severity and frequency of such actions.
  • 2.4 Toxicity. Toxicity is not allowed on this server. Toxicity is the act of continuously using racial slurs, sexual harassment or severely explicit language in a way that drives players away from the community. Toxicity is declared on a case-by-case basis by staff. First offenders will be warned or otherwise contacted by staff to let them know when they have broken this rule. Repeated violations may lead to warnings, mutes and eventually a permanent ban.
  • 2.5 Impersonating other players, towns and nations. . Impersonating players by taking advantage of, but not limited to, usernames, nicknames, town/nation names is not allowed. Offenders will be asked to revert to their proper character. If any damage is done staff may choose to ban the offending player.
  • 2.6 AFK farming. Standing in a farm “away from keyboard” is currently allowed provided that no other rules are broken in the process (see rule 1.1). You may be kicked if server needs to free up space and resources for actively playing users.
  • 2.7 Sharing personal information. Sharing personal information and identities of others without their explicit permission is strictly forbidden. Personal information may be, but not limited to, “real life” photos of players, phone numbers, home addresses, school/work location, family relations and IP-addresses.
  • 2.8 Sharing links Malicious links are not allowed. Links that contributes or aid in breaking any of the rules listed on this page are not allowed. Links to adult content is not allowed. Other links are allowed.
  • 2.9 Advertising. Advertising is not allowed unless it’s related to the server. Advertisers will be muted.
  • 2.10 Selling content and services for real money. You are not allowed to sell in-game items or services for real life money.
  • 2.11 Sending false support tickets and lying to staff. You are not allowed to make fraudulent support tickets or lie to staff in support matters. Lying to staff applies to any support matter, whether in support tickets or elsewhere. Sending invalid support tickets is also not allowed as of rule 2.3.

3. Griefing

  • 3.1 Griefing and destroying land around claims. Players may not damage terrain or structures around or in claims against the owners will, this act is referred to as griefing and is not allowed on this server. Players breaking this rule will be warned and damage will be rolled back. Players doing this more than once or if the grief is considered major may face a permanent ban. Please note that mayors/kings are responsible for misconfigured town/nation permission settings. Examples of grief:
    • Cobble monsters - Mountain-like structures that are built using lava and water.
    • Lava/water curtains - Placing rows of lava/water in the sky to make a wall of falling lava/water.
    • TNT craters.
    • Mass spawning entities within or around a town (Chickens, Boats, etc.).
  • 3.2 Destroying land in wilderness. Wilderness is defined by Towny as any land not currently claimed. You are allowed to grief structures or terrain as long as they are not within or in close proximity to a claimed area. However, any player may request grief to be rolled back even in the wilderness.
  • 3.3 Claim blocking. “Claim blocking” is referring to the act of deliberately placing outposts or towns near another town to block its expansion. This is not allowed. This rule is in place to ensure that all players have room to play on the map. Players reporting a claim block must have a sincere intention to expand in that direction and are expected to do so after blocking claim is removed. There are three possible scenarios when it comes to claim blocking:
    • Outpost blocking town: In this case the outpost will be removed, town expansion is prioritized since each player can have many outposts but only one town. Staff will remove the outpost if certain requirements are met. Town claims must be right up against the outpost. In addition, if the outpost was placed before the town was funded there must also be things built in the claim right up against the outpost to the center of the town. If this isn’t the case staff finds it hard to justify that you really need the outpost claim.
    • Town blocking another town: In general, each town has equal access to claim nearby land and staff will therefore not intervene. Europe is a good example of this, barely any land is left yet claims are not removed. However, staff may act if a town is deliberately placed for the sole purpose of blocking or otherwise harass neighboring town. Amongst other reasons, staff will consider towns that claim around other towns, towns that are inappropriately close to another town in relation to the space available a bit further away, towns that have few residents but rapid expansion close to another town and towns that are claimed in unnatural shapes. It’s up to staff to conclude whether a town is blocking another town. Staff will contact the mayor and ask the mayor to move. Staff may or may not refund the town depending on the situation.
    • Outposts on abandoned towns: Placing outposts over abandoned towns is also considered claim blocking, these areas are supposed to be left for new mayors to claim.
  • 3.4 Stealing specific blocks. Stealing specific blocks for their value is not considered grief and is therefore allowed. This does not apply to easily acquired blocks even if they are valuable to you.

How and where do I appeal my punishment?

You have the chance to appeal for any punishments provided by staff. To do so visit our dedicated discord server for appeals found here (this server is different from our main discord server). After stating the reason for your ban and your in-game username you will be provided a text channel where you will be able to explain yourself and the situation to staff.

How do I report a player breaking the rules?

Please join our main discord server here and start a new support ticket by running the command “-new”. A separate text channel will be provided named after your ticket number. You do not have to wait for staff to reply here, instead provide as much evidence as you have of the player and which rules are broken from the list above.

Who is a player and who does above rules apply to?

Everyone playing on EarthMC Minecraft server or anyone part of our discord server is a player. Players cover all ranks.

Who is staff?

Anyone who holds the Mod, Admin and Owner rank is considered staff. Plugin and modpack developers are not staff although they may have access to your support ticket if it’s relevant to your issue.

How do I report staff abuse?

EarthMC has zero tolerance for staff abuse. Please contact Fix#0001 or KarlofDuty#5275 through discord and provide as much evidence as possible.
