Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will you transfer data from an account to another account of mine?

No, we are not able to transfer any data between accounts.

2. How do I apply for moderator?

Apply here. https://goo.gl/Ssc9U2.

3. How do I switch between chats?

/gc -for global chat
/tc -for town chat
/nc -for nation chat
/pc -for party chat
/dc -for donator chat

4. Why are mobs acting weird, not moving?

Certain mob AI is turned off to improve the server performance:

Blaze

5. I voted but I did not get my gold. Can I get a refund?

No, the issue is on the side of the voting sites and nothing we can change.

6. How can I link my Minecraft account with my Discord account?

Type the following command in-game and follow the instructions.

/discordsrv:discord link

7. My town got deleted, why?

Players are deleted from towns after 42 days of inactivity.
