FAQ

What is Premium?

EarthMC Premium is a subscription service for a low monthly cost. Premium gives you access to the fastest queue priority, cosmetics and a bunch of awesome stuff.

How much does Premium cost?

EarthMC Premium costs $7.99 per month. No extra costs, no contracts.

What is a Premium collectible?

Collectibles are limited cosmetic items that can only be acquired through Premium.

There are no contracts and no commitments. You can easily cancel your Premium subscription on your paypal account dashboard in a few clicks. There are no cancellation fees, start or stop your Premium access at any time.
